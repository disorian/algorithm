## üí° Tips for Success

- **Consistency over intensity**: Regular practice (1-2 hours daily) beats marathon sessions
- **Understand, don't memorise**: Focus on understanding patterns, not memorising solutions
- **Write clean code**: Practice writing production-quality C# code, not just solutions that work

- **Explain your solutions**: Teach concepts to solidify understanding
- **Review others' solutions**: Learn different approaches and optimisations
